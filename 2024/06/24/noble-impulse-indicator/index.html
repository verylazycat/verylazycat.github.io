This complimentary forex Indicator can be configured to notify you of signal alerts through email, SMS, or as platform pop-ups. This feature is advantageous as it eliminates the need for constant chart monitoring throughout the day, and facilitates the tracking of multiple charts at once.

The Impulse V4 Indicator is versatile, compatible with any Forex currency pair and various other financial assets such as stocks, commodities, cryptocurrencies, precious metals, oil, and gas. It can be applied to any time frame you prefer, although it is most effective on the M5, M15, and M30 timeframes.

The Noble Impulse Indicator system provides trading signals that can be directly acted upon or further refined through additional chart analysis for filtering, which is advisable. This system is accessible to traders of all proficiency levels, and it is recommended to practice using it on an MT4 demo account until you achieve consistent results and feel ready to trade with real funds.

Enhancements in Noble Impulse V4

  • Customizable Support and Resistance levels with three sensitivity settings
  • Volatility-based adjustable Take Profit (TP) and Stop Loss (SL)
  • Integration of the Ichimoku Cloud as a filter
  • A smart dashboard in Noble Impulse V4 for an overview of signals across various timeframes
  • A dashboard-mounted market state indicator to distinguish between trending and ranging markets

Strategies Included with Noble Impulse

  • The foundational Noble Impulse Strategy
  • The advanced strategy utilizing the Ichimoku Cloud
  • The intermediate Moving Average-based Impulse Strategy
