The Best Non-Repainting Forex Indicators for MT4 that really work

Created2024-07-05|Updated2024-07-05
|Word Count:873|Reading Time:5mins|Post Views:

Here is our well-vetted compilation of the Top 10 highest-performing non-repainting Forex indicators for the MT4 platform that have proven their effectiveness.

Itrend Averages

click to download

Our Favorites Include:

  • The non-repainting iTrend indicator stands out as one of the most genuinely lucrative trend-based trading tools we’ve encountered to date.
  • Aesthetically appealing and user-friendly, this indicator allows even inexperienced traders to quickly identify trends with just a cursory look.
  • There’s no necessity for additional confirmation instruments; the iTrend is sufficient on its own for trade entry and exit decisions.
  • It’s effective for scalping on shorter timeframes, starting from the 5-minute chart upwards.
  • This tool excels at directing traders to the commencement of a trend and signaling its conclusion.
  • The secondary level of the indicator serves as a reliable signal for scalping entries or re-engaging with ongoing trends, particularly during volatile market “spikes.”
  • It is equipped with over 40 distinct Moving Average Filters, defaulting to the ADXvma for its filtering process.
  • It performs exceptionally well across various markets, including Futures, Forex, Cryptocurrency, and Indices trading.
  • This indicator is a significant leap forward in sophistication and coding quality compared to the common iTrend variations found online, which are derived from the original iTrend concept.

Room for improvement

  • While this tool excels at identifying significant price swings, it requires the use of broader stop levels; otherwise, traders might find themselves prematurely exited from trades before they align with the prevailing trend’s direction.
  • If we are to be critical, this instrument does not offer guidance to traders on setting stop loss or take profit points.

Step NEMA

click to download

Our Praise For:

  • The NEMA (n-EMA) indicator is capable of computing up to 49 different EMA types, utilizing a maximum of 50 internal calculation buffers. It is designed to calculate various types of moving averages, including EMA, DEMA, TEMA, and others, based on the value set for the NemaDepth parameter. To put it simply, inputting “3” for NemaDepth results in a Triple Exponential Moving Average calculation, while “40” yields a Quadragintuple Exponential Moving Average.
  • It offers high precision for timing entry into swings and provides clear signals for exit and re-entry.
  • This indicator can serve as a reference for determining Stop Loss levels.
  • It includes advanced features like Multi-timeframe support with Interpolation, a variety of Display options such as “dots” and “bars”, and an Alerts system for user notifications.

Room for improvement

  • The capacity to iteratively calculate Exponential Moving Averages up to 49 times may not directly enhance trading performance and primarily serves to repeatedly smooth the EMA.
  • Certain configurations of this indicator are tailored for seasoned technical analysts. Unfortunately, there is an absence of guidance on utilizing the complete array of settings, which can perplex novice traders.

Waddah Attar Explosion Averages with ATR DeadZone

click to download

We like:

  • Performs remarkably well, particularly when optimized with these refined settings.
  • The ATR DeadZone feature provides extra validation for identifying a move characterized by volatility.
  • Notifications are now accessible, alerting users whenever the ATR DeadZone is breached.
  • Distinctive in its visual presentation, it stands out from the majority of indicators that utilize subwindow displays.
  • It aids in streamlining and decluttering the chart’s visual layout.
  • It functions effectively as a standalone system for identifying entry and exit points in trends.
  • It includes advanced features such as Multi-timeframe with Interpolation, an extensive selection of Moving Average techniques, and an Alerts system.

Room for improvement

Trend Direction & Force index

click to download

Our Approval For:

  • Its potency in trend trading.
  • The activation of the “Change Color On Zero Line Cross” feature, which provides trend traders with enhanced and clearer signals than those offered by the MACD.
  • A proven and dependable indicator that is well-suited for both scalping and initiating swing trades across all timeframes.
  • Its straightforward and user-friendly design.
  • Its high efficacy as an entry filter for the timing and execution of trades.
  • Its versatility, making it immediately usable for all trader types without the need for any customization.
  • The inclusion of Multi-timeframe with Interpolation, a Multi-symbol selection feature, and an extensive array of over 33 diverse Averages for use as smoothing filters and price indicators.

Room for improvement

  • While this indicator is dependable and provides precise entry signals, the downside is its infrequency in generating signals, which can lead to a scarcity of trading chances (particularly noticeable when the tool is utilized in Multi-timeframe mode). This might result in frustration due to infrequent opportunities.
  • It may present a challenge for novice traders who could often find themselves scrutinizing the median line closely in an attempt to discern the onset of a legitimate trade entry.

Chaos Visual Averages

Our Positives Include:

  • Exceptional precision and capacity to predict market peaks and troughs even at the most minute timeframes.
  • Aesthetically appealing and straightforward visualization of market states.
  • Robust outcomes from back-testing analyses.
  • Notification features.
