4. Cookie Usage

We offer the option to save your name, email address, and website in cookies when you comment on our site. This is for your convenience during future comments, eliminating the need to re-enter your information. These cookies are valid for one year.

Upon visiting our login page, a temporary cookie is set to test your browser’s cookie acceptance. This cookie is devoid of personal data and is removed when your browser is closed.

Upon logging in, several cookies are established to retain your login information and your display preferences. Login cookies are active for two days, while display option cookies last for one year. Choosing the “Remember Me” option extends your login duration to two weeks. Logging out removes the login cookies.

When editing or publishing content, an additional cookie is stored in your browser, indicating the ID of the content you last edited. It is devoid of personal data and expires after one day.

5. External Embedded Content

Our site’s articles may contain embedded content from other sources, such as videos, images, and articles. Such embedded content functions identically to visiting the originating website.

These third-party websites may collect data about you, utilize cookies, and track your interaction with the embedded content, including any actions taken while logged into their services.
